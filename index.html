Side Panel Demo

This is a demonstration of a sliding side panel. Click the hamburger menu button in the top-left corner to open the navigation panel.

Features

  • Smooth slide-in/slide-out animations
  • Responsive design that works on all devices
  • Overlay background when panel is open
  • Easy to customize and integrate
  • Clean, modern design

How to Use

  1. Click the menu button (☰) to open the side panel
  2. Click anywhere on the overlay or the close button (×) to close
  3. Navigate through the menu items
  4. The panel is fully responsive and works on mobile devices
